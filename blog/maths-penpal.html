Maths Penpal: The AI Tutor That Speaks Your Language

Posted: December 1, 2025 | Project Started: November 2025

Education is evolving, and Maths Penpal is at the forefront of this transformation. Designed specifically for the Queensland curriculum (ACARA v9.0), this application isn't just another quiz app—it's an intelligent, voice-activated companion that grows with the student.

A New Way to Learn

Maths Penpal leverages the power of Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash model to create a seamless, conversational learning experience. Instead of dry text-based questions, students interact with "Aura" or "Robert"—AI personas that can explain concepts, offer encouragement, and adapt to the student's pace.

Key Features

  • Voice-First Interaction: Speak naturally to your AI tutor. Whether you need help with algebra or just want to chat about your progress, the conversation flows in real-time.
  • Curriculum Aligned: Content is strictly aligned with ACARA Mathematics for Years 5-10, ensuring that practice is always relevant to schoolwork.
  • Adaptive Difficulty: The system intelligently scales questions from "Easy" (Simple Familiar) to "Hard" (Complex Unfamiliar) based on performance.
  • Gamification: Earn points, unlock trophies, and complete daily challenges to keep motivation high.

Under the Hood

Built with React and the latest Web Audio API, Maths Penpal demonstrates the potential of low-latency AI interactions. The application processes voice input locally and streams responses instantly, creating an illusion of a live human connection.

The Vision

Our goal with Maths Penpal is to democratize access to high-quality, personalized tutoring. By combining the empathy of a penpal with the precision of an AI engine, we are making maths not just understandable, but enjoyable.
